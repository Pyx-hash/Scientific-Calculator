Calculator Functions

This scientific calculator support basic and advanced mathematical operations including:

  • Basic Aritmetic: +, -, *, /
  • Advanced Functions: sin, cos, tan, and their inverses
  • Logarithms: log (base 10), ln (natural)
  • Contants: π (pi), e (Euler's number)
  • Other Operations: √ (square root), ^ (exponent), ! (factorial), % (percentage)
  • Memory Functions: MC (Memory Clear), MR(Memory Recall), M+(Memory Add), M-(Memory Substract)

Currency Converter

The currency converter allows you to convert between 8 major world currencies. You Can:

  • Use the calculator value by clicking "USe Calculator Value"
  • Swap currencies with the ⇆ button
  • Manually set exchange rates or fetch live rates from the internet
  • See the last update time for exchange rates

3D Mode & Settings

Enable 3D Mode for an immersive experience with depth effects and parralax tilting. You can also:

  • Toggle between light and dark themes
  • Enable or disable button click sounds
  • Your preferences are saved between sessions
